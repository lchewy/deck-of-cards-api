Shuffle Your Deck

The following endpoint uses the Fisher Yates shuffle algorithm to shuffle or reshuffle your deck

.../api/shuffle_deck

Get Specific Suits

Get all cards from a specific suit from your current hand with .../api/{SUIT}

{SUIT} should be either diamonds, clubs, hearts or spades.

Sort Your Deck

No matter how randomize your deck becomes you can sort your current with the following endpoint

.../api/sort_deck

Deck will come back as Diamonds [A-K], Clubs [A-K], Hearts [A-K] and Spades [A-K]
